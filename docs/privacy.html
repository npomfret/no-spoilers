Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

No data collected

No Spoilers does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics.

Network requests

The app fetches publicly available Formula 1 race schedule data from an open-source repository at github.com/sportstimes/f1. No user information is included in or derived from these requests.

No third-party services

The app does not use advertising, crash reporting, or any third-party SDK that could collect data.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/npomfret/no-spoilers.
